# Sermon Illustration Prompt

Create a church sermon illustration for the theme "He Is Not Far From Us." Show a group of believers walking together on a rough, rocky path, symbolizing the journey of life with its struggles and challenges. One person is visibly tired and almost falling, while two fellow believers support and lift them with strong, loving hands. Near them, a warm and gentle light glows close by, symbolizing God's presence that stays near and never abandons His people.

The image should communicate closeness, mutual support, gratitude, steadfast faith, and the commitment to keep following God in every situation. The atmosphere should feel hopeful, reverent, and encouraging, with a cinematic composition and clear emotional storytelling.

Style: semi-realistic digital painting, warm tones, soft dramatic lighting, high detail, church presentation quality.
Format: horizontal 16:9.
No text, no watermark, no logo.
